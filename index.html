Create an account

Account type

Once you have opened a personal account or business account, you will be able to add up to 5 people as authorised users to help you manage your account. Each user will be given unique sign in credentials.

What's the difference between a personal and business account?

There is little difference between these account types.

With either account you can nominate up to 5 account users to help manage it. Each user will get their own sign in credentials.

Personal account users can set up Auto Pay, an automated payment service for up to 5 vehicles. Suitable for:

  • Resident's discount
  • Blue Badge holders
  • 9+ seater discount
  • Cleaner vehicle discount
  • Motor tricycles
  • Roadside recovery vehicles

Business account users can set up Auto Pay or Fleet Auto Pay, an automated payment service for companies with a fleet of 6 or more vehicles. Also suitable for accredited breakdown vehicle discount.
